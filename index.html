Top Ten Favorite Vegan Cheeses
  1. Violife Mozzerella Shreds

    2. Tastes great on pizza and melts almost like the real thing, no harm involved. Truly a superior cheese.

  2. Forager Queso Fresco

    3. Adds a perfect gooey texture to enchiladas. Chef's kiss.

  3. Miyoko's Garlic Herb Cheese

    4. Perfect cheese for a charcuterie board alongside some mushroom jerky.

  4. Daiya Key Lime Cheezecake

    5. The only acceptable product from Daiya and yes cheesecake counts as cheese, don't @ me.

  5. Babybel Plant Based Cheese

    6. We sampled this once at Whole Foods and my boss said, "They got the flavor right, but the texture feels like rubber." I couldn't tell, but I also don't remember what animal cheese feels like, so whatever.

  6. 365 Vegan Mozzerella

    7. Speaking of WFM, this store brand cheese is a good, cheap substitute for my number one pick, the Violife mozz. It's perfect if you're still a week out from payday but need to use up that dough ball in your freezer.

  7. Treeline Goat Cheese

    8. Another great one for your cheese board.

  8. Feild Road Chao Slices

    9. A perfect grilled cheese go-to. I reccommend the Tomato Cayenne flavor.

  9. Nuts for Cheese Un-Brie-Lievable

    10. Authentic tasting cheese to pair with wine and feel fancy. They also make a vegan bleu cheese, but tbh I haven't tred it and I'm a lil nervous to.

  10. Follow Your Heart Parmesan

    11. Another pizza party must-have. The first vegan cheese brand I ever tried, Follow Your Heart will always have a special place in my fridge.
